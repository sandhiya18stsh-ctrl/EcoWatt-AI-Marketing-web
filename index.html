About EcoWatt AI

Smarter energy, powered by intelligence

EcoWatt AI is on a mission to make every kilowatt count. We use advanced machine learning to read your electricity bills, understand how your home or business consumes power, and recommend exactly how to spend less without compromising comfort.

By detecting peak-hour patterns, predicting future costs, and surfacing inefficient appliances, our platform turns raw energy data into clear actions — making sustainability simple, accessible, and financially rewarding for everyone.

💡 Energy Saving Tips

  • Turn off idle appliances at the plug.
  • Use heavy devices during optimal off-peak hours.
  • Replace old appliances with energy-efficient models.
  • Monitor your monthly usage to spot anomalies early.
